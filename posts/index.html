25 White Elephant Gift Ideas

This post contains affiliate links to Amazon. What’s a “White Elephant” gift? It’s a silly gag gift usually for playing a White Elephant game! How does it work? There’s a pile of gifts, one per person who is playing. Everyone draws a number which decides the order they choose a gift from the pile. If you have 13 people/13 gifts, you’ll have numbers 1-13 to draw from. Starting with person #1, they will choose a gift from the pile to unwrap and reveal to everyone.
